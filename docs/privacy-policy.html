Privacy Policy

App: Snake Game

Package name: com.snake_game_agent_test.app

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Snake Game is an offline game. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use analytics, and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Data stored on your device

The app stores recent game scores and the best score locally on your device. This information is used only to show score history inside the app. It is not sent to the developer or to any third party.

Data collection and sharing

The app does not collect personal or sensitive user data. The app does not share user data with third parties.

Data retention and deletion

Local score data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear the app's storage from Android system settings.

Security

Because the app does not transmit user data, personal data is not sent over a network. Score history is stored locally using Android app storage.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at https://github.com/yuyao-wang/snake-android/issues.
