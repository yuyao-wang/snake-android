## Full Description

Snake Game is a minimalist arcade game designed for focus and relaxation.

Swipe to guide the snake, collect food, and build your score at your own pace.
The clean black-and-white interface keeps the experience simple and calm, with
no ads, no account, and no distractions.

Your recent scores are saved on your device so you can return later and keep
improving.

## Key Features

- Minimalist black-and-white design
- No ads
- Swipe gesture controls
- Focused, distraction-free gameplay
- Local recent score history
- Offline play
